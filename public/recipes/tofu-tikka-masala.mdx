# Tofu Tikka Masala
Tofu Tikka Masala is a vegan spin-off of the classic Chicken Tikka Masala. It's a flavorful Indian curry made with marinated and fried tofu that's cooked in a delicious masala paste along with aromatic spices.

## Ingredients:
- Tofu
- Garam masala
- Turmeric
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Oil

## Instructions:
1. Put half the oil into a large pan and set over a medium-high heat.
2. Add the tofu, half the garam masala and half the turmeric, 1 teaspoon of salt and Â½ teaspoon of black pepper.
3. Toss everything together and fry for 5 minutes until golden and fragrant.
4. Transfer the contents to a bowl and return the pan to the heat.
